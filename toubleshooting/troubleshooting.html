Wi-Fi wont connect- Troubleshooting Guide

Problem: Your computer can't connect to the Wi-Fi network

Step-by-Step

  1. Check Wi-Fi is turned on
    Windows:Click the Wi-Fi icon on the taskbar and make sure the Wi-Fi is enabled
    Mac: Click the Wi-Fi icon on the menu bar and check it is turned on
  2. Verify Airplane mode is off
    Airplane Mode disables the wireless connections.Make sure it is off
  3. Restart your computer and router
    Sometimes a simple restart fixes connection glitches
  4. Check your Wi-fi network
    Can other devices connect to the Wi-Fi?
    If no, reboot the router or check the outages with your ISP.
  5. Forget and reconnect to the Wi-Fi network
    On your computer "forget" the network and reconnect by entering the password again.
  6. Update Wi-Fi drivers(for Windows)
    Go to Device Manager> Network Adapters> your Wi-Fi device> Update driver.
  7. Try connecting to a different Wi-Fi
    This helps check if the issue is with your device or the network

Why these steps help:

  • Turning the Wi-Fi on/off resets the connection.
  • Airplane mode disables wireless radios
  • Restarting clears temporary bugs.
  • Forgetting and reconnecting resets network settings
  • Updating drivers ensures compatibility and bug fixes.
  • Testing another Wi-Fi isolates whether the problem is your device or the router.
