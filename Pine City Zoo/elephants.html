ELEPHANTS

Elephants are large mammals of the family Elephantidae and the order Proboscidea. Two species are traditionally recognised, the African elelphant(Loxodonta africana) and the Asian elephant(Elephas maximus). Elephants are scattered throughout sub-Sahara Africa, South Asia and Southeast Asia. Male African elephants are the largest extant terrestrail animals and can reach a height of 4m(13ft) and weigh 7,000kg(15,000lb).

Elephants are herbivorus and can be found in different habitats including savannahs,forests,deserts and marshes.They prefer to stay near water.
